What do you use?

This website is hosted on a Vultr VPS, running Arch Linux.

When did you make this?

I made this website in December 2022, being inspired by Luke Smith's LandChad project. Initially it was meant only to host a small homepage and a Nextcloud server, but I've since expanded it with the sections in the navbar.

No social links?

While this might seem as a radical choice, I consider that having a personal website is vastly superior to having n social media accounts. I still have some social media, such as my YouTube channel, but I scarcely use it nowadays.

How can I contact you?

You could email me at andrewstephen870@gmail.com.

I plan on implementing a chat feature for this website in the future.

Your site is very simple!

I find minimalism to be very attractive. This website might not win any contests, but it is, in my opinion, very comfy and fast.

Arch on a server? Really?

I chose Arch on a server due to the minimal footprint in terms of packages and vast documentation provided by the ArchWiki. Additionally, bleeding-edge packages are very useful, in spite of potential breakage.
